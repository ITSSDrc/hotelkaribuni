# **App Name**: Karibuni Oasis

## Core Features:

- Room Showcase: Visually present each room with high-quality images and detailed descriptions.
- Booking Engine Integration: Enable direct booking through a secure and user-friendly interface.
- AI-Powered Recommendations: Suggest personalized activities or room upgrades based on guest preferences using an AI tool.
- Gallery of Experiences: Feature guest experiences through photos and testimonials to build trust.
- Interactive Map: Display a map of the surrounding area with points of interest marked.
- Multilingual Support: Offer the site in French, with easy options to expand to other languages.
- Contact and Inquiry Form: Allow visitors to easily contact the hotel for inquiries or special requests.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vivid orange (#FF7F50) to evoke warmth and energy.
- Background color: Very light orange (#FAF9F6) to provide a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFB347) for call-to-action elements.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', sans-serif for a clear and readable user experience
- Headline text: 'Playfair', serif for titles and important headings
- Use elegant line icons for navigation and to highlight key features.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout to ensure content is well-organized and easily accessible.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
